Product Description

Our grass-fed ribeye is sourced from local farms where cattle graze freely on natural pastures. The generous marbling through this cut delivers exceptional flavour and tenderness whether you pan-fry, grill, or reverse-sear to your preferred doneness.

Each steak is hand-cut by our master butcher to approximately 250g and dry-aged for a minimum of 21 days to develop deep, complex flavour. Best served medium-rare with a resting period of at least 5 minutes.

Storage: Keep refrigerated at 0–4°C. Consume within 2 days of collection or freeze on day of collection.
