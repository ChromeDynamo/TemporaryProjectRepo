Our Story

StreetDoko was born out of a simple frustration — great local traders, but no easy way to shop from them without queuing. We set out to build a platform that puts the quality of the local market in your pocket, letting you browse, order, and collect on your own terms.

We work exclusively with independent traders who share our values: provenance, freshness, and real craft in what they do.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make it easier for communities to support their local economy. Every order placed on StreetDoko goes directly to an independent trader — no middlemen, no supermarket mark-ups.

We believe that the best food is grown, raised, and prepared close to home — and we're building the tools to make that the easiest choice you can make.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Click & Collect work? ▼
Browse products, place your order, choose a collection slot (Wed–Fri), and collect at the market hall.
How far in advance do I need to book? ▼
Orders must be placed at least 24 hours before your chosen collection slot.
Can I order from multiple traders at once? ▼
Yes — add from any of our five traders into a single basket and check out once.
How do I become a trader? ▼
Register using the Trader tab on the registration page. Our admin team reviews applications within 3 working days.
